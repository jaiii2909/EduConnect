Introduction to UI/UX Design

UI (User Interface) design focuses on the look and layout of a product's interface, while UX (User Experience) design is about the overall feel, functionality, and satisfaction a user experiences. Together, they create seamless and enjoyable digital interactions.

Why UI/UX Design Matters

  • Improves user satisfaction and engagement
  • Boosts conversion rates and business success
  • Reduces development costs through efficient design
  • Builds brand trust and loyalty
